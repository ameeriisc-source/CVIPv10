About the Conference

The International Conference on Computer Vision & Image Processing (CVIP) is an established annual conference series that brings together researchers from academia and industry working in the areas of vision, imaging, and artificial intelligence.

CVIP 2026 is hosted by the National Institute of Technology Calicut (India) and jointly organized with the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Norway.
