National Institute of Technology Calicut

NIT Calicut is one of India’s premier technical institutions, located amidst the scenic Western Ghats. The campus provides an ideal environment for academic interaction and research collaboration.

Kozhikode is a historic coastal city known for its role in global maritime trade, cultural heritage, and academic prominence.

Wayanad is renowned for its misty hills, biodiversity, wildlife sanctuaries, and waterfalls. Kerala, often referred to as “God’s Own Country,” offers rich cultural traditions and sustainable tourism experiences.
